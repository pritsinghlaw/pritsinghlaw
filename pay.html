Pay Your Bill Securely

We make it easy for you to pay your legal fees securely online. Our preferred payment platform, LawPay, is designed for law firms and ensures your payment information is handled with the highest level of security.

Preferred: LawPay

Our primary payment processor, specifically designed for legal services. LawPay ensures compliance and provides bank-level security for all transactions.

  • Accept credit and debit cards securely
  • eCheck payment options available
  • Compliant with legal industry standards
  • Instant payment processing

Alternative: Stripe

We also accept payments through Stripe. Contact our support team if you prefer to use this option or need assistance with setting up a payment plan.

How to Pay Your Bill:

  1. Click the "Pay Now" button below.
  2. You'll be securely redirected to our payment processor.
  3. Enter your payment information and submit.
  4. You'll receive a confirmation receipt via email.

Security Notice: All payments are processed through encrypted, secure connections. We never store your payment information on our servers.
