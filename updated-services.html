Real Estate Legal Solutions You Can Trust

Strategic representation and expert legal counsel across litigation, transactions, disputes, and property law across California — tailored to protect your investments and safeguard your interests.

Real Estate Litigation

We represent clients facing disputes involving property sales, development agreements, or complex real estate partnerships, guiding them strategically through trial or settlement.

  • Strategic litigation plans customized to each dispute
  • Thorough analysis of contracts, evidence, and risks
  • Proactive pursuit of settlement or decisive trial action
  • Comprehensive support through appeals and judgment enforcement

Landlord / Tenant Matters

Our attorneys handle eviction proceedings, lease violations, rent disputes, and habitability claims, safeguarding landlords’ investments and tenants’ rights.

  • Efficient management of eviction and lease compliance issues
  • Effective negotiation to reduce conflict and expense
  • Expert guidance on local housing laws and regulations
  • Swift legal action to restore rights or protect property

Premises Liability

We protect property owners from unjust liability claims and aggressively represent injured clients seeking compensation for dangerous property conditions.

  • Investigation to pinpoint responsibility and liability
  • Negotiation of fair settlements to limit courtroom exposure
  • Expert handling of insurance claims and litigation strategies
  • Vigorous representation at trial if settlement isn't possible

Boundary Disputes

Our firm resolves neighbor disputes involving fence placement, property lines, encroachments, and survey inaccuracies, restoring peace and clarity.

  • Clear boundary determination backed by thorough research
  • Skillful mediation to quickly defuse neighbor tensions
  • Representation in boundary litigation if needed
  • Enforcement of judicial orders for lasting resolution

Quiet Title Actions

We resolve disputes over property ownership, removing clouds on title from liens, inheritance challenges, recording mistakes, or adverse claims.

  • Detailed title examinations to clarify ownership status
  • Swift judicial actions to eliminate competing claims
  • Supportive guidance through the quiet title legal process
  • Securing marketable title efficiently for refinance or sale

Adverse Possession Claims

We help clients defend against or pursue legal ownership claims based on long-term use, occupancy, and property maintenance.

  • Thorough evaluation of adverse possession criteria
  • Assertive litigation to secure or challenge possessory claims
  • Strategic negotiation for quick dispute resolution
  • Enforcing or contesting adverse possession in court

Easements and Encroachments

We assist property owners with disputes over access rights, driveway use, utility easements, and unauthorized property encroachments.

  • Legal analysis of easement scope and validity
  • Effective negotiation to realign property use rights
  • Litigation to defend or contest easement rights if necessary
  • Securing favorable settlements or court-enforced orders

Mortgage Fraud

Our attorneys defend homeowners and investors from deceptive lending practices, forged documents, and fraudulent real estate schemes.

  • Comprehensive forensic review of mortgage transactions
  • Aggressive litigation to recover losses or rescind fraudulent loans
  • Strategic engagement with regulatory authorities
  • Securing remedies for victims of mortgage-related fraud

Foreclosure Defense

We represent homeowners threatened with foreclosure, challenging unfair practices by lenders and advocating for loan modifications and relief.

  • Rapid action to halt wrongful foreclosure proceedings
  • Negotiation of affordable loan restructuring
  • Assertive litigation to protect homeowner rights
  • Representation at foreclosure mediations and trials

Contract Review & Drafting

We craft and evaluate real estate contracts, leases, and related agreements to safeguard client interests and prevent future disputes.

  • Customized agreements tailored to specific client needs
  • Careful drafting to reduce ambiguity and risks
  • Proactive legal advice to anticipate contractual issues
  • Ongoing counsel to ensure continued enforceability

Purchase Agreements

Our firm guides clients through complex property transactions, drafting or reviewing purchase agreements to protect their investments.

  • Comprehensive review of contractual terms and conditions
  • Negotiation to secure favorable agreement terms
  • Assistance in navigating transaction contingencies
  • Enforcement of rights when transactions face complications

Real Estate Closings

We manage real estate closings efficiently, resolving last-minute title issues, contractual disputes, and ensuring smooth property transfers.

  • Thorough preparation to identify and resolve closing obstacles
  • Efficient handling of document review and title verification
  • Rapid intervention when unexpected disputes arise
  • Facilitation of successful property transfers and closings

Property Broker Disputes

Our attorneys advocate for brokers, buyers, and sellers in commission conflicts, fiduciary breaches, and allegations of misconduct in real estate transactions.

  • Clear analysis of broker obligations and contractual rights
  • Resolving commission conflicts through negotiation or litigation
  • Vigorous defense against claims of broker negligence
  • Enforcement of brokerage agreements and professional standards

Property Financing Documents

We prepare, review, and negotiate mortgages, promissory notes, and other financing instruments to ensure compliance and protect client interests.

  • Precise drafting tailored to each financing situation
  • Expert negotiation of lending terms favorable to clients
  • Comprehensive legal compliance checks
  • Support in resolving lending or default disputes swiftly

Title & Escrow Disputes

Our firm assists clients in navigating disputes arising from escrow mismanagement, withheld funds, or errors in title documentation.

  • Prompt investigation to uncover escrow and title irregularities
  • Negotiation to quickly release or recover escrowed funds
  • Legal advocacy to ensure accountability from escrow agents
  • Securing clear titles and escrow disbursement efficiently
